Invisible Bankruptcy: Small Costs, Big Destruction

"It's just a few dollars a week, what's the harm?" This sentence is the biggest obstacle to financial freedom. Gambling is a sneaky addiction.

Opportunity Cost: What Are You Losing?

Spending money on lottery tickets has a huge opportunity cost. You are losing the chance to invest that money elsewhere.

Spending $10/week on Lottery:

1 Year: $520
10 Years (No Interest): $5,200
10 Years (Invested): ~$8,000 - $10,000+

With this money, you could have paid for education, a dream vacation, or started a small business. Instead, you have nothing but paper trash.

The Dopamine Trap

Our brains are weak against "uncertain rewards". Gambling triggers the reward center just like social media. The "Near Miss Effect" keeps you playing even when you lose.
